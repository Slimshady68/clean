General cleaning

This involves routine cleaning tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, sweeping, mopping, and surface wiping to maintain cleanliness in residential or commercial spaces.

Deep cleaning

This type of cleaning involves more thorough and detailed cleaning, targeting hard-to-reach areas, corners, and spaces that accumulate dirt and grime over time. It may include cleaning carpets, upholstery, windows, blinds, and other surfaces.

Disinfection and sanitization

Cleaning companies may offer specialized services for disinfecting and sanitizing surfaces, especially in settings that require a high level of hygiene, such as hospitals, healthcare facilities, and food establishments.

Specialized cleaning

Cleaning companies may provide specialized services tailored to specific industries or environments. This can include industrial cleaning, post-construction cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, or cleaning services for events and occasions.
